My personal interests

I've always been fascinated by coding and the endless possibilities it offers. Writing lines of code feels like constructing a complex puzzle, where each piece must fit perfectly for the final product to function. There's something incredibly rewarding about debugging an issue and watching your creation come to life on the screen.

When I'm not coding for class or working, I love spending time playing video games. I enjoy exploring vast digital worlds, whether I'm embarking on an epic adventure or challenging friends in competitive matches. Video games provide both an entertaining escape and a way to connect with people who share my passion for gaming.

Another big interest of mine is Dungeons & Dragons (DND). I'm drawn to the imaginative storytelling and the collaborative nature of the game, where each player's decisions can shape the entire campaign. From rolling dice to crafting detailed backstories for my characters, DND allows me to dive into a realm of creativity and shared adventure.
